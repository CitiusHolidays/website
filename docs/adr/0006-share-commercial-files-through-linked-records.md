# Share commercial files through linked records

Commercial files attached to a Query, Proposal, or Job Card are exposed read-only throughout that linked Commercial Record Chain from their original source rather than copied between records. This gives Sales, Contracting, Ticketing, and Operations one consistent document set without duplicate versions, while passport, visa, expense, finance, and HR documents retain their separate sensitive-access boundaries; only a user authorized to manage the source record may delete its file.
